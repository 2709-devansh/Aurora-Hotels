SOCIAL WORK

AURORA  WORKS

CLOTH
DONATION

"The value of life is not in its duration, but in its donation."

We donate the old bedsheets, towels, hand Sprays, hand Towels, even old beds pillows, etc of our every hotel, in whichever corner of the country it might be. It is done every year to the poor and orphanges.

FOOD
DISTRIBUTIONS

“Money is meaningless if you cannot put food in the poors stomachs.”

We give the left over food to the needy and poor people every day in every place we are able to go. We have served over a thousand people in every hotel to the people who are really in need of it.

SOLAR
PANELS

"Solar power is the last energy resource that isn't owned yet - nobody taxes the sun yet."

Using the natural source of Enery : the Sun ,to run our most of the Electronics we save hundreds of units of electricity. using this we are able to save energy which can then be provided to the villages.

TREE
PLANTATION

“If you cut a tree, you kill a life. If you save a tree, you save a life. If you plant a tree, you plant a life.”

We along with our visitors have planted together around 1,00,000 trees. We have a campus with a very apealing natural look and even wildlife like monkeys, birds and ponds with small fishes too.

RAINWATER
HARVESTING

"Rainwater Harvesting is much better than unnecessary water wasting."

Water is essence of life, thus we must save rainwater. We save water in every rain that happens in the location with our advanced rainwater harvesting systems.

WASTE
COMPOSTS

"The ground's generosity takes in our compost and grows beauty! Try to be more like the ground."

We have tried to make a nature campus and even have made composts using the vegetable waste and are continuously doing so....
